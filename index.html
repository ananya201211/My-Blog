Review on My Favourite Movies

  Zootopia
Excellent film for animal and comedy lovers

It was a very good family animation, but it was also a timely parable about tolerance, a bunny cop movie, a pinpoint parody of bunny cop movies and a riff on a wide range of crime films and a parody of Disney's own oeuvre. It was genuinely funny, but it was also the best conspiracy thriller in a very long time.

  Ratatouille
Excellent film for animal and food lovers

The story is heartwarming and relatable, with Remy's passion for cooking and determination to pursue his dream despite the obstacles he faces. The voice cast, led by Patton Oswalt as Remy, is spot on and brings the characters to life in a way that is both endearing and entertaining.
